Grant Proposal to Apple and ConnectED Initiative for Brightwood Elementary School Funding (PDF)
This 21-page grant proposal was completed for Introduction to Professional Writing with Professor Thomas Pickering at University of Massachusetts Amherst in the fall of 2018. The sample proposes the donations of Apple products to Brightwood Elementary School through the Apple and ConnectED Initiative. The proposal provides information on poverty in Springfield, as well as the potential budget of the project.

Food Ethnography (PDF)
This 11-page food ethnography was written for Expository Writing in the fall of 2018 at University of Massachusetts Amherst. This outlines issues in the diet industry through the lens of one nutrition store, GNC. The ethnography also includes how social media, diet trends, and family traditions influence how we eat.
